What do I need to do?

So now you've considered your priorities and exlored your options, you might be readty to make a decision.

Your pension savings will need to support you through retirement, whick for some people can be more than 30 years. It's important you cinsider all your options carefully and make the right choice for you.

AGE 50+

Prep: Explore your options - use this site to understand the options that might be available to you.

1-6 MONTHSBefore Retirement

1. Request your retirement pack(s) from your scheme administrator(s).

1-6 MONTHSBefore Retirement

2. Guidance call/advice - Arrange your guidance call to understand the implications of the different options for you. If required, obtain advice.

MAKE YOUR
DECISION

3a. A regular income for life - Shop around for annuity.

3b. Invest your savings and withdraw them as and when you need - Choose the right drawdown arrangement for you.

3c. Cash lump sum - Contact your scheme administrator to arrange.

It's worth noting that the different retirement options have different timescales attached.


Annuity: The average time from the annuity broking request to completing the annuity purchase is 76 days

Drawdown: Can take 1-2 months after the completed forms have been received to transfer your account

Cash: Can take up to a month after the completed forms have been received to pay the cash into your account
